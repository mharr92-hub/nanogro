## Campaign Guardrails

- Do not present pending, inferred, or estimated data as verified.
- Do not invent ROI, yield, dates, dosage, farm names, farmer names, certifications, lab results, or control data.
- Use quantified claims only when they are present in the source case.
- For cases marked "Review before publishing" or "Evidence needed", use the copy as draft creative only until the technical team approves the case.
- Add this footer on technical/result posts: "Resultados observados en casos documentados. Los resultados pueden variar segun cultivo, manejo y condiciones de campo."

## Designer Handoff Notes

Recommended formats:

- Feed post: 1080 x 1350.
- Carousel: 5 to 7 slides.
- Story/Reel cover: 1080 x 1920.

Suggested carousel structure:

1. Hook: crop + result or technical angle.
2. Problem: drought, transplant stress, germination, disease pressure, low production, or vigor.
3. Nano-Gro application: only show dosage when verified in the case.
4. Evidence: original technical report, field photographic record, table, or testimonial.
5. Result: quantified only if present in source.
6. Disclaimer: "Resultados observados en casos documentados. Los resultados pueden variar segun condiciones de campo."
7. CTA: "Solicita una evaluacion tecnica."
